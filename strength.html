What is Strength Training?

Strength training involves exercises that improve muscular strength and endurance by working against resistance. It is essential for building muscle mass, improving metabolism, and enhancing overall physical health.

Benefits of Strength Training

  • Increases muscle mass and strength
  • Improves metabolism and fat burning
  • Enhances bone density
  • Boosts functional fitness for daily activities
  • Improves posture and reduces risk of injury

Popular Strength Training Exercises

Weightlifting

Weightlifting involves using free weights like dumbbells and barbells to target various muscle groups and build strength.

Bodyweight Exercises

Bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and pull-ups are effective for building strength without needing equipment.

Resistance Bands

Resistance bands are versatile tools that provide constant tension during exercises, making them great for strength training.

Functional Training

Functional training uses exercises that mimic daily movements to improve strength, balance, and coordination.
